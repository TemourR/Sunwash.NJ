Services

  • Ceramic Coating Application:

    Sunwash NJ takes car protection to the next level with our premium ceramic coating service. This advanced nano-coating forms a durable shield over your vehicle’s paint, providing exceptional resistance to UV rays, oxidation, bird droppings, and minor scratches.

    Benefits:

    Long-Lasting Shine: Ceramic coatings create a glossy, mirror-like finish that lasts for years. Hydrophobic Properties: Water beads up and rolls off effortlessly, making washing a breeze. UV Protection: Shield your car’s paint from harmful sun rays and fading. Reduced Maintenance: Say goodbye to frequent waxing; ceramic coatings require minimal upkeep.

Stain Removal: Say farewell to coffee spills, pet accidents, and mystery stains. Odor Elimination: We neutralize unpleasant smells, leaving your car smelling fresh. Dashboard and Trim Restoration: Bring back that factory-fresh look.
